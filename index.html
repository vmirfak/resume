"Keychain Machine"

Academic project developed in the area of ​​Industrial Automation whose objective was the design and development of an automated and robotic system for painting a logo on the faces of aluminum sheets. This system involves a feeding station for previously cut sheets, a station for drilling the hole to place the ring, a robotic station for painting the logo on both sides of the plate and a conveyor that ensures the movement of the product between all seasons in work. The system consists of an ABB industrial robot, pneumatic cylinders and respective valves, sensors, a drilling machine, a tactile console and an autotamo.

"Design and Manufacture of a Plastic Injection Mold"

Curricular project developed in the areas of Mechanics, Metalworking and Materials for the design of a mold of plastic injection requested by the company in the municipality of Águeda, Fig-Fábrica de Plásticos, Lda. This project involved the study, design, conception and manufacture of the mold for the manufacture of hinges in Polyamide 6. For the construction of the mold it was necessary to study the area of ​​plastic injection, perform technical drawings of the part, design the mold and all its constituents, manufacture the mold and test the molding cycle. The modeling of the part and all the constituents of the mold he was carried out using the SolidEdge ® software, and for the analysis and evaluation of the injection was using MoldFlow® and to simulation and generation of the machining processes of the parts, PowerMill® was used.

"Two Elevator Optimization System"

Curricular project developed in the areas of Digital Electronics and Electronic Instrumentation for the design and development of a model that simulates two elevators in a four-story building. The work consisted of building the model, implementing the control circuit and programming the system, and the management of the elevators was ensured by the PIC18F26K22 microcontroller, programmed in C, and by Digital Electronics. The system is composed of microswitches that simulate the buttons of call of a real elevator and end-of-course microswitches to provide information on the cabin location. Elevators work automatically and efficiently, since when several requests are made simultaneously, the system calculates the most short for both elevators in order to satisfy the requests.

"Project of an Electronic Harp"

Thematic project developed in the areas of Analog Electronics for the design and construction of a Electronic Harp based on infrared light beams, which simulate the strings of a real harp and an ordinary loudspeaker to emit the generated sound. Since a harp has 47 or 48 strings, we chose for building a harp with three musical notes, emitted by "strings" simulated by three beams of light infrared. By placing a hand or an object between each of the beams of light, the user causes the emission of a predefined sound of a note. As a musical note is associated with a frequency, the model has the ability to to change octave (doubling the frequency of the musical note), making the harp actually have six notes musicals.

"Project of an Air Treatment Unit"

Curricular project developed in the areas of Fluid Mechanics and Thermal Machines with the aim of sizing, designing, building and testing an air handling unit. One air handling unit is capable of carrying out the combined processes of heating, heating and humidification, cooling and air cooling and humidification, but as the time period for the conception of this project went through months of high temperature and humidity, the machine is only capable of cool and dehumidify the air. That said, the designed machine guarantees a temperature of 20ºC and a relative humidity of 40%.
